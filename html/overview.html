Overview

The amrutth v1.0 API enables access foodtrucks data with relevant search results. The data is provided by DataSF Food Trucks . Why is the name amrutth? What does it mean? amrutth means food/drink which confers immortality.

Users of v1.0 will be able to:

  • Find up to the 100 best results for a geographically-oriented search
  • Sort results by the distance or best match
  • Limit results to those foodtrucks offering specific type of food, status of permit etc.
  • search a specific foodtruck by name
